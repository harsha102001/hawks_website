About Mysuru Hawks Club


Mysuru Hawks Baseball Club was founded with a single vision — to build a strong, passionate, and disciplined baseball community in the heart of Mysuru. What began as a small team of dedicated athletes has grown into a thriving club that fosters talent, teamwork, and sportsmanship.
Our club is home to players of all ages who are united by a love for the game and a drive to excel. Whether on the training field or in competitive leagues, the Mysuru Hawks represent hard work, resilience, and the spirit of rising higher — just like the hawk in our name.
We are committed to developing the next generation of baseball stars, promoting fitness and focus, and putting Mysuru on the map in the world of baseball. Every swing, every pitch, every catch — it's not just a move, it’s our mission.

Our Club Mission:


Our mission is to build a strong, disciplined, and passionate baseball community in Mysuru. We are committed to nurturing young talent through structured coaching programs for Under-13 and Under-16 age groups. Through expert training, team spirit, and a focus on fundamentals, we help players grow in skill, confidence, and character. Mysuru Hawks is more than just a club — it’s a launchpad for young athletes to learn, compete, and rise as future champions. We strive to promote baseball across Mysuru while instilling values of respect, leadership, and dedication in every player.
